# MindFrame Video Production Specifications

## Platform Overview
All content is produced for **9:16 Vertical Video**.

### 1. TikTok
- **Duration:** 30-90 seconds.
- **Pacing:** High-velocity. Cut every 1.5 - 2.5 seconds.
- **Safe Zones:** Keep text within the center 70% to avoid being covered by the UI (Like/Share/Caption).
- **Audio:** Trending stoic/ambient sounds at 10% volume under the voiceover.

### 2. Instagram Reels
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds (preferred).
- **Pacing:** Cinematic and smooth.
- **Visuals:** High-contrast, "aesthetic" B-roll.
- **Audio:** Original Audio with the MindFrame branding.

### 3. YouTube Shorts
- **Duration:** Under 60 seconds (Hard limit).
- **Pacing:** Very fast. Hook must happen in the first 1.5 seconds.
- **SEO:** Title should include a viral hook from the database.

## Visual Identity & Assets
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16).
- **Typography:**
  - Font: Archivo Black or Montserrat Extra Bold.
  - Color: White text, occasionally highlighted with Gold (#DAA520).
  - Shadow: Soft black drop shadow for readability over complex B-roll.
- **Text Overlay Positions:**
  - Primary text: Center-middle or slightly above center.
  - CTAs: Bottom-middle (above the caption area).

## B-Roll Styles
- **Cinematic Stock:** Dark academia, minimalist architecture, urban rain, stoic statues, macro nature (texture-focused).
- **Dynamic Elements:** Light leaks, subtle film grain, dust overlays.
- **Motion:** Always use slow-motion (60fps interpreted to 24fps) or slow pans. No shaky cam.

## Voiceover Specifications
- **Voice:** Deep, resonant, masculine (e.g., ElevenLabs "Adam" or "Antoni" with adjusted stability).
- **Delivery:** Stoic, deliberate, pauses for effect after "Truth Bombs."
- **Clarity:** Ensure zero background noise. Voice should sound "in the room."

## Editing Protocol
1. **The Hook:** Match the visual to the verbal hook exactly.
2. **The Body:** Use jump cuts between points. No transitions like fades or wipes.
3. **The Subtitles:** Large, single-word or short-phrase captions that appear in sync with the speech.
4. **The CTA:** Clear, static text on a black or dark grey background for the final 3 seconds.
