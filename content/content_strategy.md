# MindFrame Content Strategy

## Overview
MindFrame's content is designed to disrupt the 'doom-scrolling' cycle with high-impact, stoic, and psychological insights. The goal is to move viewers from passive consumption to active implementation (and eventually into our digital product ecosystem).

## Platforms
- **TikTok/Shorts/Reels:** Primary discovery engine. Focus on high-retention hooks and cinematic aesthetics.
- **Email Newsletter:** The 'Deep Dive'. Where we bridge the gap between inspiration and implementation.

## Content Lifecycle
1. **Discovery:** Short-form video with a viral hook.
2. **Engagement:** CTA drives to a 'Free Guide' or 'Lead Magnet'.
3. **Monetization:** Email sequences upsell to 'Productivity Templates' or the 'Premium Membership'.

## Visual Style Guidelines
- **Aesthetic:** 'High-Performance Noir'. Dark backgrounds, gold/white accents, minimalist typography.
- **B-Roll:** High-quality stock footage (Pexels/Envato/Midjourney-generated). Slow motion, macro shots.
- **Audio:** Deep, resonant AI voiceovers (ElevenLabs). Low-fi or dark ambient background tracks.

## Automation Integration
The templates in `templates/content_templates.json` are designed to be parsed by AI agents to generate scripts. The `hooks/viral_hooks_database.json` should be used to split-test different openings for the same body content.
