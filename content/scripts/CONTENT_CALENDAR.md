## Platform-Specific Optimization

### 1. TikTok
*   **Hashtags:** #productivity #stoicism #discipline #mindset #motivation #highperformance
*   **Strategy:** Reply to the first 10 comments within 30 minutes to signal engagement to the algorithm.

### 2. Instagram Reels
*   **Hashtags:** #mindframe #deepwork #stoicmindset #productivitytips #wealthmindset #personaldevelopment
*   **Strategy:** Share to Story with a "Poll" sticker (e.g., "Do you check your phone in the morning? Yes/No") to drive engagement.

### 3. YouTube Shorts
*   **Hashtags:** #shorts #productivity #success #discipline
*   **Strategy:** Pin a comment with a link to the "MindFrame OS" Notion template. Use a clear, curiosity-gap title.

## Engagement Protocol
1.  **First 60 Mins:** Monitor all platforms. Heart and reply to every comment.
2.  **Conversion:** If a video hits >10k views, add a "Hard CTA" in the comments linking to the Premium Membership.
3.  **Analytics:** Review every 7 days. Double down on the pillar with the highest retention.
