# MindFrame Affiliate Program Structure

## Goal
To leverage our community to drive new members and customers while rewarding them for their loyalty.

## 1. Commission Tiers
- **Digital Products (Ebooks, Vaults):** 40% per sale.
- **Premium Membership:** 30% recurring (lifetime of the subscriber).
- **Automation Packages ($297):** 20% ($60 per referral).

## 2. Payout Terms
- **Threshold:** $50 minimum payout.
- **Schedule:** Paid out on the 1st of every month via PayPal or Wise.
- **Cookie Window:** 60 days.

## 3. Affiliate Assets (The "Affiliate Kit")
- **Video Library:** 20+ viral-style raw clips for TikTok/Reels with "no-face" B-roll.
- **Swipe File:**
    - High-converting Twitter/X threads.
    - Email templates for newsletters.
    - Instagram Story templates.
- **Brand Guide:** Hex codes, logos, and fonts to ensure consistent aesthetics.

## 4. Software Recommendation
- **Gumroad:** For digital products (built-in affiliate system).
- **Rewardful:** For the Stripe-based membership (integrates with Skool/Circle).

## 5. Promotion Guidelines
- **Ethical Marketing:** No false claims about earnings or results.
- **Disclosure:** Must include #ad or #affiliate in social posts.
- **Community First:** Focus on the value of the systems, not just "making money".
