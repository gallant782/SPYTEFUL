# Lead Magnet Spec: The 5-Minute Daily System

## Overview
- **Title:** The 5-Minute Daily System
- **Subtitle:** A minimalist daily protocol to reclaim your focus, leverage AI, and achieve more by doing less.
- **Format:** PDF Cheat Sheet + Resource Template Pack (Notion/Google Sheets).
- **Target Audience:** High-performers, early-career professionals, and students overwhelmed by productivity "noise."

## Core Content Outline
1. **The Morning Pivot:** A 120-second daily intention-setting ritual (80/20 rule application).
2. **AI Synergy Protocol:** 3 specific prompts to automate your daily schedule and research tasks.
3. **The Deep Work Trigger:** A checklist of environmental and mental cues to enter flow state in under 5 minutes.
4. **The Shutdown Sequence:** A 2-minute end-of-day routine to eliminate "mental loops" and switch off.
5. **The Resource Pack:**
   - Focus Tracker (Notion Template).
   - "Gold Standard" AI Prompt Library (10 high-leverage prompts).
   - Recommended toolstack (MindFrame approved).

## Visual & Design Brief
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, sleek, high-contrast (Dark mode options).
- **Brand Colors:** Charcoal, Neon Lime (Accent), White.
- **Mockup:** 3D rendering of a tablet showing the PDF and a phone showing the Notion template.
- **Layout:** One-page "at-a-glance" cheat sheet + detailed 5-page companion guide.
