# MindFrame Premium Membership Structure ($19/mo)

## Core Value Proposition
"Stay at the forefront of AI and productivity without the overwhelm. We curate the tools, build the systems, and connect you with the winners."

## 1. The Content Pillars
- **The Automation Vault:** A searchable library of n8n, Make.com, and Zapier blueprints. Users can import these directly into their own accounts.
- **The Prompt Lab:** Categorized database of "Gold Standard" prompts for business, content, and personal growth.
- **Weekly Pulse:** A members-only newsletter sent every Sunday summarizing the 3 most important AI tools of the week and how to use them.

## 2. Community Experience
- **Platform:** Skool (recommended for gamification and ease of use).
- **Channels:**
    - #introductions (Onboarding)
    - #wins (Social proof & motivation)
    - #ai-tools (Sharing new finds)
    - #systems-help (Support for technical workflows)
    - #general-chat (Networking)

## 3. Events & Engagement
- **Monthly Build-Along:** A live session where we build an automation from scratch based on member requests.
- **Office Hours:** 1-hour monthly Q&A with the MindFrame team.
- **Challenges:** 30-day "Content Sprints" or "Deep Work Challenges" with leaderboard prizes.

## 4. Onboarding Sequence (Automation)
1. **Immediate:** Welcome email with login credentials and "Start Here" video.
2. **Day 2:** Guide to "The Vault" (How to use the blueprints).
3. **Day 7:** Invitation to the next live Build-Along.

## 5. Retention Strategy
- **Annual Discount:** $190/year (Get 2 months free).
- **Referral Program:** Refer 3 members, get your membership free for life.
- **Badges:** Earn status based on community contributions.
