# Lead Magnet Specification: The MindFrame OS (Notion Template)

## Hook
"Stop organizing your life. Start automating your success. Download the MindFrame OS — the only Notion system designed for the AI era."

## Core Value Proposition
A frictionless system to manage tasks, capture knowledge, and leverage AI to get 4 hours of work done in 1.

## Structure
1. **The Command Center:** Daily "Focus" view using the 80/20 rule.
2. **AI Prompt Library:** 50+ pre-built prompts for content creation, research, and problem-solving.
3. **The Second Brain:** A high-leverage knowledge capture system (PARA method adapted for AI).
4. **Project Pipeline:** Track personal and professional goals with automated progress bars.
5. **Growth Lab:** Tracking habits, reading lists, and Skill Acquisition.

## Delivery Method
- Notion Template link (Shared upon email confirmation).
- Video walkthrough (3 mins) on the Thank You page.

## Conversion Goal
2% opt-in rate from short-form content traffic.

## Assets Needed (Collaboration with Product Creator)
- Notion Template development.
- Mockup images of the template on Desktop/Mobile.
- Landing Page copy (see `emails/landing_page_copy.md`).
