| Platform | Weekdays | Weekends | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

## 5. Cross-Posting Workflow
1.  **Original Edit:** High-quality 9:16 vertical video (no watermarks).
2.  **Primary Upload:** Post to TikTok at 7 PM.
3.  **Secondary Upload:** Post to Reels (use a different trending audio if applicable).
4.  **Tertiary Upload:** Post to YouTube Shorts.
5.  **Stories:** Share the Reels/TikTok to IG Stories with a "LINK IN BIO" sticker.

## 6. Community Engagement (First 30 Days)
*   **Reply to EVERY comment** for the first 30 days to build the "MindFrame Tribe."
*   **Pin one comment** on every video with a direct CTA (e.g., "The link to the Daily System is in my bio for the next 24 hours only.")
*   **Search relevant hashtags** (#productivity, #notiontips) and leave 10 thoughtful comments daily on other creators' posts.
