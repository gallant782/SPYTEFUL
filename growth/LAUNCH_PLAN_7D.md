# 7-Day Launch Plan: "MindFrame Genesis"

## Goal
Go from zero to the first 500 leads and $1,000 in revenue.

## Timeline

### Day 1: Infrastructure & Funnel Sync
- **Systems:** Final end-to-end test of the n8n automation for Lead Magnet (5-Minute System) delivery.
- **Commerce:** Verify Stripe/Gumroad checkout for "The Productivity Vault" ($27).
- **Checklist:**
    - [ ] Landing page forms capturing emails correctly.
    - [ ] Redirect to Thank You page with Tripwire offer working.
    - [ ] Abandoned cart email trigger verified.

### Day 2: The First Impression (Launch Day)
- **Content:** Post Batch 01 (3 videos) across TikTok, IG Reels, and YT Shorts.
- **CTA:** "Free 5-Minute System in bio."
- **Engagement:** First 3 hours after posting: respond to every comment to boost algorithmic reach.

### Day 3: Data-Driven Iteration
- **Analytics:** Review retention graphs for Day 2 videos.
- **Iteration:** Refine hooks for Day 4 content based on what held attention.
- **Email:** Send Welcome Email 1 ("The Delivery") to all Day 2 leads.

### Day 4: Value Explosion
- **Content:** Post 2 "How-to" videos demonstrating one specific AI prompt from the resource pack.
- **Email:** Send Email 3 ("The AI Advantage") — educate on the power of prompts and soft-pitch the $27 Vault.

### Day 5: The Genesis Offer (Scarcity)
- **Strategy:** Launch a 48-hour "Genesis Discount" ($27 -> $17) for the Productivity Vault.
- **Social:** Post stories on IG/TikTok announcing the limited-time discount for "founding members."
- **Email:** Send Email 5 ("The Offer") highlighting the discount.

### Day 6: Proof & Authority
- **Social:** Post mockups of the "MindFrame OS" and user feedback (if any) or "Behind the Scenes" of the build.
- **Urgency:** Final 24-hour countdown for the Genesis Discount.

### Day 7: The Debrief
- **Audit:** Calculate Followers, Leads, and Sales.
- **Standardization:** Transition the Vault back to $27.
- **Growth:** Schedule the first "MindFrame Pulse" bi-weekly newsletter.

---

## Launch KPIs
- **Total Leads:** 500+
- **Tripwire Conversion:** >3% (15+ sales)
- **Follower Growth:** 1,000+ total across platforms
