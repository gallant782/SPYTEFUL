# 90-Day Scaling Roadmap: "MindFrame Momentum"

## Vision
Transform MindFrame from a new brand into a dominant authority in the AI-productivity space with a self-sustaining revenue engine.

---

## Month 1: Foundation & Optimization
**Goal:** 10,000 Followers | $2,000 Total Revenue

### Content Strategy
- **Velocity:** 2 videos per day across TikTok/IG/YT.
- **Variety:** 70% Viral Hooks (Growth), 30% System Walkthroughs (Authority).
- **Action:** Content Architect to refine the "Batch Production" system to stay 7 days ahead.

### Growth & Conversion
- **LP Optimization:** A/B test the Lead Magnet landing page to maintain a >20% opt-in rate.
- **Lead Nurture:** Fully implement the bi-weekly "MindFrame Pulse" newsletter.
- **Feedback:** Conduct a survey of the first 100 customers to refine "The Productivity Vault" messaging.

---

## Month 2: Recurring Revenue & Community
**Goal:** 50,000 Followers | $5,000 MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)

### Product Expansion
- **Membership Launch:** Open **MindFrame Premium ($19/mo)**.
- **Onboarding:** Product Creator to finalize the "Automated Onboarding" flow for new members.
- **Retention:** Launch the "Success Coaching" bot (n8n + OpenAI) for community members.

### Ecosystem Growth
- **Referral Engine:** Activate the "Refer 3, Get Free" program for membership.
- **Partnerships:** Outreach to 10 micro-influencers for affiliate-only launches.
- **Paid Testing:** Begin "Boosted Post" testing with a $20/day budget on high-performing organic reels.

---

## Month 3: Authority & High-Ticket Leverage
**Goal:** 100,000 Followers | $10,000+ Total Monthly Revenue

### Monetization Scale
- **High-Ticket Offer:** Launch **"The Automation Accelerator" ($297)**.
- **Sales Funnel:** Implement a webinar or long-form video funnel specifically for the high-ticket package.
- **Upsell Path:** Optimize the "Productivity Vault" checkout to include a 1-click upsell to the Membership.

### Operations & Scaling
- **Team Expansion:** Evaluate the need for a dedicated Video Editor or Community VA.
- **Paid Ads:** Scale winning ad sets to $100/day if ROI > 3x.
- **Brand Authority:** Launch the MindFrame Podcast (repurposed from Weekly Live Builds).

---

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 2 | Month 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
