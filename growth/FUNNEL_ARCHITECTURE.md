# MindFrame Funnel Architecture

## 1. Awareness & Traffic Acquisition (TOFU)
- **Primary Channels:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts.
- **Content Strategy:** High-retention, faceless AI-generated videos focusing on productivity psychology, "high-leverage" habits, and AI automation.
- **Micro-Conversion:** Content View → Profile Visit.
- **Call to Action (CTA):** "Steal my 5-minute daily system (Link in Bio)".

## 2. Lead Capture (MOFU)
- **Profile Link:** Directed to a high-converting Landing Page (see `LANDING_PAGE_SPEC.md`).
- **The Hook:** "The 5-Minute Daily System" — a PDF cheat sheet + template pack.
- **Action:** User submits email address via an embedded form (Beehiiv/ConvertKit).
- **Redirection:** User is redirected to a "Thank You" page with a one-time "Tripwire" offer ($27 Productivity Vault).

## 3. Email Automation & Nurture (BOFU)
- **Welcome Sequence (5 Days):** (See `EMAIL_SEQUENCES.md`).
- **Abandoned Cart Flow:** If the user initiates checkout for the Tripwire or Membership but doesn't finish:
    - **Hour 1:** Friendly reminder + "Did you lose your link?".
    - **Hour 24:** Benefit focus + "What you're missing out on".
    - **Hour 48:** Final call + 10% discount code.
- **Nurture Loop:** Bi-weekly "MindFrame Pulse" newsletter providing free value, AI tool reviews, and soft-selling the Membership.

## 4. Product Ecosystem (The Value Ladder)
1. **Free Lead Magnet:** "The 5-Minute Daily System" (PDF/Cheat Sheet).
2. **Tripwire Offer ($27):** "The Ultimate Productivity Vault" (Deep-dive templates and Notion workspaces).
3. **Core Subscription ($19/mo):** MindFrame Premium Membership (Community, weekly live builds, exclusive AI prompts).
4. **Backend Upsell ($97-$297):** "Automation Accelerator" — Done-with-you setup of custom AI workflows.

## 5. Post-Purchase & Referral
- **Fulfillment:** Immediate email with access credentials/download link.
- **Retention:**
    - Day 1: "Welcome to the Inner Circle" (Invite to Skool Community).
    - Day 14: "How's your progress?" (Checking in on the system).
- **Referral Ask (Day 30):** "Love the system? Share it with a friend and get your next month free." (Link to affiliate portal).

## 6. Metrics & KPIs
- **Content-to-LP Conversion:** >10%.
- **LP Opt-in Rate:** >20%.
- **Lead-to-Tripwire Conversion:** >3%.
- **Tripwire-to-Membership Conversion:** >10%.
- **Churn Rate:** <8%.
