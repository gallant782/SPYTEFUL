# MindFrame Funnel Architecture

## 1. Top of Funnel (TOFU) - Awareness
- **Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts.
- **Content:** Faceless motivational/psychology videos with strong hooks.
- **Goal:** Drive traffic to the link in bio.
- **Call to Action (CTA):** "Download the Free MindFrame Operating System (Link in Bio)" or "Join 5,000+ high-performers in our newsletter (Link in Bio)".

## 2. Middle of Funnel (MOFU) - Consideration
- **Asset:** Landing Page (simple, high-conversion).
- **Offer:** Free Lead Magnet (The "MindFrame OS" Notion Template).
- **Goal:** Capture email address.

## 3. The Value Ladder (Products & Upsells)
- **Free:** The MindFrame OS (Lead Magnet).
- **Tripwire ($27):** The Ultimate Productivity Vault (Expansion pack for the OS).
- **Order Bump ($9):** The Viral Hook Database (Add-on for content creators).
- **Core Offer ($19/mo):** MindFrame Premium Membership (Community + Weekly Tools).
- **High-Ticket Upsell ($297):** Automation Pro Package (1-on-1 workflow setup + custom blueprints).

## 4. Bottom of Funnel (BOFU) - Conversion
- **Sequence:** 5-day "Automated Mindset" Email Series.
- **Day 1:** Deliver Lead Magnet + Welcome.
- **Day 2:** The Psychology of Productivity (Value).
- **Day 3:** The "Digital Leverage" Concept (Value + Soft Pitch for $27 Vault).
- **Day 4:** Case Study/Proof (Result-oriented).
- **Day 5:** Hard Pitch for MindFrame Premium Membership ($19/mo).

---

## Technical Stack Requirements (Coordination with Systems Engineer)
- **Landing Page:** Framer or Vercel-hosted React app.
- **Email Service Provider (ESP):** Beehiiv or ConvertKit (integrated via n8n).
- **Checkout:** Gumroad or Stripe.
- **Database:** Supabase for tracking subscriber journey and conversion metrics.
